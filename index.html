Hello World: Welcome to my website!

My name is Jubal Rife. I am a Software Developer in Billings Montana.
I graduated from Rocky Mountain College in 2014 with a Bachelor's degree of Computer Science. Shortly after graduation I began working professionally as a full-stack developer. Over the years my role has transitioned from Junior Developer, to Senior Developer, to my current position as Director of Engineering.
I have spent my whole professional life working in the same legacy codebase (financial technology, full-stack, over a million lines of code, 100+ microservices many of which are third-party integrations) with largely the same collegues (we're a midsized team of around 10 people. We've had very little turnover so I am working with a lot of the same collegues from years ago) It has been a rich learning experience as we have grown together professionally and roles have shifted over time. I feel honored to work with such excellent people.
When I'm not working on software, I like to make music, read, sketch, occasionally even do creative writing. (As well as many other less-bragworthy forms of entertainment)
Thanks for dropping by. I hope you find something of interest here.
